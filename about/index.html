About Us

Lara's Gifts is made up of Seta Karkoudrian (owner), Maral Malatian (order fulfillment) , and Lucy Ouzounian (chief designer). We have specialized in favors since 1986.

We've handled wedding favors, bridal shower favors, baby shower favors, christening and baptism favors and have extensive knowledge in nearly all types of special family occasions.

Trust our experience and vast product selection to make your next occasion the best and most memorable it can be!

We can also help you create custom favors. Recent favors include Victorian themed favors and scented candles wrapped beautifully in tulles. We are full of good ideas!
